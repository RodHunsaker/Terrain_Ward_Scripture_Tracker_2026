2026 Iron Rod Scripture Reading Tracker

A simple, clean, offline-capable scripture reading tracker created for members of the **Terrain Ward** for the 2026 scripture reading challenge.  
This digital tracker helps you stay consistent, build meaningful habits, and visually track your progress throughout the year.

Features

Daily Scripture Tracking  
Tap each day of the calendar to mark your reading as completed. Your progress automatically updates in real time.

Streak Tracking  
The tracker shows:
- Your current reading streak  
- Your longest streak  
- Total days completed  
- Percentage of the year finished  
Youâ€™ll also see streak badges as you reach key milestones.

Dark Mode  
Switch between light and dark mode anytime. Your preference is saved automatically.

Progress Saving (Offline)  
Your progress is stored securely on **your own device**. 
Nothing is uploaded, shared, or synced anywhere else.

Even when offline, the app:
- Loads instantly  
- Keeps all your progress  
- Works like a regular installed app

Installable App (PWA)  
This tracker can be added to your home screen on:
- iPhone or iPad  
- Android phones  
- Tablets  
- Computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)
Once installed, it launches full-screen with its own app icon.
